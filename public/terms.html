1. Service Description

DropBeam is a browser-to-browser local-network sharing utility that allows users to temporarily share text clipboard clips and upload files up to 100MB per file.

All shared data is temporary: files expire and are permanently deleted after 15 minutes, or immediately upon download if self-destruct is enabled. Chat messages and live clipboard contents are held in volatile memory and purged upon disconnect.
