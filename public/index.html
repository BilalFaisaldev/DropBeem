How It Works

DropBeam operates over your local network interfaces, providing swift browser-to-browser transfers.

1

Connect on Wi-Fi

Ensure all devices you want to share with are connected to the exact same Wi-Fi or local network.

2

Open DropBeam

Go to the sharing link or scan the QR Code on your mobile to load the dashboard in your room.

3

Sync & Drop

Paste text inside the Live Clipboard or drop your files. Other connected devices see them instantly!

Core Features

Engineered for speed, security, and universal device support.

WebSocket Live Sync

Updates occur in real-time. No manual browser reloading is ever required to fetch text clips or file drops.

Private Room Codes

Switch from the public sharing board. Lock your room with passcodes to secure critical files from neighbors.

15-min Expire Timer

Files are cached temporarily in local storage and deleted forever after 15 minutes, preserving your privacy.

Self-Destruct Downloads

Enable self-destruct mode. Files are deleted from both the browser list and server disk immediately after download.

Multi-Device Support

Runs directly in modern web browsers. No app downloads are needed for iOS, Android, Windows, or Mac.

Real-time Room Chat

Send links, instructions, and messages to other devices in the same room using the integrated Chat board.

Your Data Is Protected

DropBeam is built on privacy-first design principles. We don't track your information or collect personal details.

No Database Storage

Text snippets and files are held in temporary memory or sandbox storage. We never log your data permanently.

Strict Deletion Policies

Once the 15-minute timer expires, files are completely unlinked from server records and physically deleted.

Local Wi-Fi Encryption

Files travel across your local router subnet, keeping your uploads secure from the public internet.

Security & User Safety

All rooms (except custom passcoded ones) are isolated to devices that connect from the same Wi-Fi router subnet. This means external users cannot access your local sharing boards or intercept your files.

To ensure maximum safety, always create a passcode-locked room when using public Wi-Fi networks in coffee shops, hotels, or airports.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about DropBeam? Find your answers here.

Do I need to install any app to use DropBeam?

No app installation is required! DropBeam runs entirely in your web browser. Just open the local URL, share the link, or scan the QR Code on your mobile to connect instantly.

Is my file data secure?

Yes. DropBeam transmits your files locally over your Wi-Fi network interface. Uploaded files are stored in a temporary sandbox directory and automatically deleted after 15 minutes. For public Wi-Fi networks, you can password lock your rooms.

How long do files and text clips stay shared?

Files remain available for download for 15 minutes before they automatically expire and are purged from disk. You can also enable "Self-Destruct" to delete them immediately after the first download. Live Clipboard text stays active until overwritten or manually cleared.

What is the maximum file size limit?

The default upload limit is set to 100MB per file upload. You can share multiple files concurrently.

Does DropBeam require internet access?

No internet access is required for local network transfers! As long as all devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi router, they can communicate locally. Internet access is only required to load external assets like font files and icons.

Is DropBeam free to use?

Yes, DropBeam is 100% free. We use non-intrusive, compliant Google AdSense advertisement banners to help fund our hosting and local server development.
